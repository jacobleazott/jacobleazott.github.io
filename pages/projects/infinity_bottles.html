Infinity Bottles

About

An "infinity bottle" is a blend of spirits that evolves over time as it is consumed and replenished. With each pour and addition it becomes a unique and personal blend, a story of not just what you've drunk, but where you've been, what you've been through, and ultimately, who you are today.


There are no strict rules for managing your infinity bottle, but generally, it's filled with the last remaining ounces of liquour from a bottle. It's reserved for special occassions, making sure the bottle is never emptied. This way, even if the bottle sees use over the next hundred years a few molecules from that very first addition will still be there.


I have two bottles that I started in 2023, a rum and a whiskey one. Both are exceptional in their own right and I could never play favorites (it's 100% the rum bottle). These bottles are filled with memories, memories of those who gifted me the bottles, memories of the places where I bought them, and memories of enjoying them with people who matter most.
